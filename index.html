Application information

  1. Reset: Press "Reset todos" to reactivate the application.

  2. Add: Write a todo-text and press "Add" (ensure that "Todo list" is selected in the rolldown).

  3. Replace: Replace a todo by writing a new todo-text, select a todo number "#" to replace (in the rolldown), then press "Add".

Current time zone: Central European Time (CET) [Daylight saving time]
